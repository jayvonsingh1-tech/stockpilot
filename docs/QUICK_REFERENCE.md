# 🎯 StockPilot Quick Reference Card

## 📊 Daily Screening (7 AM) vs 🚀 Trading Signals (Market Hours)

---

## 📊 DAILY SCREENING SUMMARY

### 🕐 When: 7:00 AM UK (Every weekday)

### 🏦 Account: **STOCKS & SHARES ISA**

### ⏰ Action: **ADD TO WATCHLIST** (Don't buy yet!)

### 📝 Steps:
1. ⭐ Add top 5 stocks to watchlist
2. 👀 Monitor for 1-2 weeks
3. 💰 Buy when price dips or shows strength
4. 📈 Hold for 3-12 months
5. 💵 Sell at +20% to +50% gain

### 💰 Investment:
- **Amount**: 5-10% of portfolio per stock
- **Risk**: Low (long-term)
- **Stop Loss**: Not needed
- **Timeframe**: 3-12 months

### ✅ DO:
- Add to watchlist immediately
- Monitor price movements
- Buy in 2-3 portions
- Hold through small dips
- Diversify across 5-10 stocks

### ❌ DON'T:
- Buy immediately
- Use CFD account
- Day trade these
- Panic sell on dips
- Use leverage

---

## 🚀 TRADING SIGNALS

### 🕐 When: During market hours (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET)

### 🏦 Account: **CFD ACCOUNT**

### ⏰ Action: **BUY IMMEDIATELY** (Within 24 hours)

### 📝 Steps:
1. 🔴 Open CFD account NOW
2. 🔍 Search for ticker
3. 💰 Set entry price (exact)
4. 🛑 Set stop loss (MANDATORY!)
5. 🎯 Set take profit
6. ✅ Place order within 24 hours
7. 📊 Monitor for 1-7 days
8. 💵 Exit at TP or stop loss

### 💰 Trading:
- **Amount**: 1-2% of portfolio per trade
- **Risk**: Medium (short-term)
- **Stop Loss**: ALWAYS required
- **Timeframe**: 1-7 days

### ✅ DO:
- Act within 24 hours
- Set stop loss immediately
- Follow timeframes strictly
- Take profits at targets
- Exit if stop loss hits

### ❌ DON'T:
- Wait or delay entry
- Skip stop loss
- Hold past max date
- Move stop loss down
- Average down on losses

---

## 🎯 How to Tell Them Apart

### 📊 Screening Message Says:
```
📊 DAILY STOCK SCREENING RESULTS
🎯 WHAT TO DO: Add to WATCHLIST
📊 Stocks & Shares ISA
⏰ Buy within 1-2 weeks
```

### 🚀 Signal Message Says:
```
🚀 TRADING SIGNAL: BUY AAPL
⚡ CFD TRADE - BUY NOW!
💰 Entry: $150.00
🛑 Stop Loss: $147.00
⏰ IMMEDIATELY - Enter within 24 hours
```

## 🆘 Emergency Rules

1. **If confused**: Check if message says "WATCHLIST" or "BUY NOW"
2. **If unsure**: Ask in Telegram or skip the trade
3. **If losing**: Stop loss will protect you (for CFD trades)
4. **If winning**: Take profits at targets
5. **If stressed**: Start with smaller amounts

## 📞 Quick Help

- **Not sure which account?** Look for "CFD" or "Stocks & Shares ISA" in message
- **Not sure when to buy?** "BUY NOW" = immediate, "WATCHLIST" = monitor first
- **Not sure when to sell?** Screening = months, Signals = days
- **Made a mistake?** Close position and learn for next time
