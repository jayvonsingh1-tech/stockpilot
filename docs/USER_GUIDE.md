# 📚 StockPilot User Guide

## 🎯 Understanding Your Daily Notifications

StockPilot sends you **TWO TYPES** of notifications. It's crucial to understand the difference!

---

## 📊 Type 1: Daily Screening Summary (7:00 AM UK)

### What is it?
Every morning at 7 AM, you receive a list of the **top 5-10 stocks** that scored highly across multiple metrics.

### 🏦 Which Account?
**STOCKS & SHARES ISA** (NOT CFD!)

### ⏰ When to Buy?
**NOT immediately!** These are for your **WATCHLIST**.

### 📝 What to Do:

1. **Add to Watchlist** (Don't buy yet!)
   - Open Trading 212 app
   - Search for each ticker (e.g., AAPL)
   - Tap the ⭐ star icon
   - Repeat for top 5 stocks

2. **Monitor for 1-2 Weeks**
   - Watch price movements
   - Look for dips or consolidation
   - Check news about the company

3. **Buy When Ready**
   - When price dips 3-5%
   - When stock shows strength
   - When you have available capital
   - Buy in 2-3 portions over time

4. **Hold Long-Term (3-12 months)**
   - These are investments, not trades
   - Don't panic on small dips
   - Target: +20% to +50% gains
   - Sell when target reached or after 6-12 months

### 💰 Investment Strategy:
- **Amount**: 5-10% of portfolio per stock
- **Timeframe**: 3-12 months
- **Stop Loss**: NOT needed (long-term)
- **Take Profit**: +20% to +50%

### ❌ DO NOT:
- ❌ Buy immediately without monitoring
- ❌ Use CFD account
- ❌ Day trade these stocks
- ❌ Use leverage
- ❌ Panic sell on small dips

---

## 🚀 Type 2: Trading Signals (During Market Hours)

### What is it?
Real-time trading opportunities sent during market hours (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET).

### 🏦 Which Account?
**CFD ACCOUNT** (NOT Stocks & Shares!)

### ⏰ When to Buy?
**IMMEDIATELY** (within 24 hours)

### 📝 What to Do:

1. **Open Trading 212 CFD Account**
   - Switch to CFD mode in app
   - Search for the ticker

2. **Place Order Immediately**
   - Use the exact entry price given
   - Set stop loss (MANDATORY!)
   - Set take profit
   - Follow the step-by-step instructions in the signal

3. **Follow Exit Strategy**
   - When TP1 hits → Take 50% profit, move SL to breakeven
   - When TP2 hits → Take 30% profit, trail SL
   - Let remaining 20% run to TP3
   - Exit by max hold date if no targets hit

### 💰 Trading Strategy:
- **Amount**: 1-2% of portfolio per trade
- **Timeframe**: 1-7 days (short-term)
- **Stop Loss**: ALWAYS set (protect capital)
- **Take Profit**: Multiple levels (TP1, TP2, TP3)

### ⚠️ CRITICAL:
- ✅ Use CFD account
- ✅ Set stop loss IMMEDIATELY
- ✅ Follow timeframes strictly
- ✅ Exit if stop loss hits (no emotions!)
- ✅ Take profits at target levels

## 🎯 Success Tips

1. **Don't Mix Strategies**
   - Screening = Long-term investing
   - Signals = Short-term trading
   - Keep them separate!

2. **Be Patient with Screening**
   - Don't rush to buy
   - Wait for good entry points
   - Buy in portions

3. **Be Disciplined with Signals**
   - Act fast (within 24 hours)
   - Always use stop loss
   - Take profits at targets

4. **Track Your Performance**
   - Use `/trades` command
   - Review weekly with `/performance`
   - Learn from wins and losses

5. **Start Small**
   - Begin with 1-2 screening stocks
   - Take 1-2 signals per week
   - Increase as you gain confidence

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I use CFD for screening stocks?**
A: NO! Screening stocks are for long-term investing in your Stocks & Shares ISA.

**Q: Should I buy screening stocks immediately?**
A: NO! Add to watchlist first, monitor for 1-2 weeks, then buy.

**Q: What if I miss a trading signal?**
A: Skip it. Don't chase. Wait for the next signal.

**Q: How many stocks should I buy from screening?**
A: Start with 3-5 stocks. Diversify across sectors.

**Q: How long should I hold screening stocks?**
A: 3-12 months. Sell at +20% to +50% or if fundamentals change.

**Q: What if a trade hits stop loss?**
A: Accept the loss. It's part of trading. Move on to next signal.

**Q: Can I hold a CFD trade longer than recommended?**
A: Not recommended. Follow the timeframe or use trailing stop.
